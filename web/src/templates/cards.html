Rules

Players take turn to move exactly one piece. Pieces can only move onto # tiles.

Small pieces (x, y) move one tile. Big pieces (X, Y) move exactly two tiles. A piece collects all tiles on its way, adding it to current player's score.

By moving, a player can make some other pieces have no valid moves. This is called "murder" and the murdered pieces are removed from the field and added to the murderer's score.

If only one player has valid moves, that player moves till the end of the game. When both players have no valid moves, the game ends. The one with larger score wins.
